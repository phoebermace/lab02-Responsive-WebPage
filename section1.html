Discovery

With a more recent discovery of 2010, the Patogititan fossils were discovered when part of a suspiciously large femur bone was found by an Argentinian farmer. Following this discovery, archaeologists began a dig that would result in more than 200 fossils being uncovered. All together the bones were estimated to come from six of the same species of dinosaur, the Patogititan that had all lived around 100 million years ago.
